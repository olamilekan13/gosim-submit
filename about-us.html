About Us

Home ABOUT US

We are thrilled to welcome you to the official website of the Gathering of Sons in Ministry (GOSIM)! Each year, we come together for a powerful summit dedicated to deepening our understanding of Christ-centered messages and providing practical support for effective ministry.

GOSIM, we believe that ministry is not just a calling but a journey of growth, learning, and fellowship. This summit is designed to equip you with profound insights, biblical teachings, and practical tools that you can implement in your ministry. Together, we will explore the depths of God’s Word and discover how to effectively reach and serve our communities.

Join us as we engage in meaningful discussions, participate in enriching workshops, and connect with fellow pastors and leaders who share your passion for serving Christ. Our time together will be filled with inspiration, encouragement, and the opportunity to forge lasting relationships within the body of Christ.

We invite you to explore our website for more information about the upcoming summit, including dates, location, speaker line-up, and registration details.
Thank you for being a part of this incredible journey. We look forward to gathering with you soon!

Mission Focus:

Pastor Akinmola’s mission work emphasizes spiritual growth and empowerment. He leads initiatives that establish church networks and support indigenous leaders. His collaborations with local churches and organizations equip communities to thrive and foster unity among them.

Impact in Southeast Asia:

In Southeast Asia, Pastor Akinmola has played a crucial role in building strong church networks and training local pastors. His efforts promote collaboration among churches, empowering them to address the spiritual and practical needs of their communities.

Impact in Africa:

In Africa, Pastor Akinmola focuses on empowering local leaders and fostering sustainable development. His mission trips equip communities with the tools they need to thrive, emphasizing selfsufficiency and spiritual growth.

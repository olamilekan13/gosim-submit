Frequently Asked Questions

Home FAQ
When does the GOSIM Summit take place?

The GOSIM Summit is held annually. The specific dates for each year’s event will be announced in advance, so be sure to check our website for updates.

Who can attend the GOSIM Summit?

The summit is open to anyone in ministry who desires to grow in the revelation of Jesus Christ. Whether you are a pastor, church leader, or someone involved in ministry work, you are welcome to join us.

Are there any fees associated with attending the summit?

There are no registration fees for the GOSIM Summit. However, attendees are responsible for their own hotel accommodations and any personal expenses related to activities outside of the event.

Can children attend the summit?

Yes, children are welcome to attend the GOSIM Summit. However, parents are responsible for arranging childcare for their children during the event.

What should I do if I have further questions about the summit?

If you have any additional questions or need more information, please feel free to contact our support team through the contact form on our website. We’re here to help!

Will there be food provided during the summit?

Refreshments will be provided at the summit, but please note that meals will not be included. We encourage attendees to explore local dining options for their meals.

Is there a schedule available for the summit?

Yes, a detailed schedule of events, including speakers and sessions, will be provided on our website prior to the summit. Be sure to check back for updates!

Can I volunteer at the summit?

Yes! We welcome volunteers who are interested in helping out at the summit. Please visit our website for more information on volunteer opportunities.

How can I stay updated on future summits?

To stay informed about future GOSIM Summits and other events, please subscribe to our newsletter on our website or follow us on our social media channels.
